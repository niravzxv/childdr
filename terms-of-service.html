  • What is a WhatsApp based mother child health telemedicine platform?

    A WhatsApp based mother child health telemedicine platform is a service that allows pregnant women and mothers of young children to access health information, advice, and care from qualified doctors and health workers through WhatsApp messages, voice calls, or video calls.

  • What are the benefits of using a WhatsApp based mother child health telemedicine platform?

    Some of the benefits of using a WhatsApp based mother child health telemedicine platform are:
    - It is convenient, as you can consult a doctor or health worker anytime and anywhere, without having to travel or wait in long queues.
    - It is affordable, as you can save on transportation and consultation fees, and also get discounts on medicines and tests if prescribed by the doctor or health worker.
    - It is confidential, as you can share your personal and medical details with the doctor or health worker without any fear of stigma or discrimination.
    - It is reliable, as you can get accurate and timely information and guidance from qualified and experienced doctors and health workers.

  • How can I access a WhatsApp based mother child health telemedicine platform?

    To access a WhatsApp based mother child health telemedicine platform, you need to have a smartphone with WhatsApp installed and an active internet connection. You also need to register with the platform by providing your name, phone number, location, and other relevant details. Once you register, you will receive a welcome message with instructions on how to use the service. You can then start sending your queries or requests to the platform's WhatsApp number, and you will receive a response from a doctor or health worker within a few minutes.

  • What kind of services can I get from a WhatsApp based mother child health telemedicine platform?

    A WhatsApp based mother child health telemedicine platform can provide you with various services related to your pregnancy and child care, such as: - Antenatal care: You can get information and advice on how to take care of yourself and your baby during pregnancy, such as nutrition, exercise, immunization, supplements, tests, etc. You can also get reminders for your antenatal check-ups and appointments.
    - Postnatal care: You can get information and advice on how to take care of yourself and your baby after delivery, such as breastfeeding, nutrition, hygiene, family planning, immunization, etc. You can also get support for any postpartum complications or issues.
    - Child care: You can get information and advice on how to take care of your child's health and development, such as nutrition, growth, immunization, milestones, common illnesses, etc. You can also get referrals for specialized care if needed.
    - Emergency care: You can get immediate assistance and guidance in case of any emergency or complication related to your pregnancy or child's health, such as bleeding, pain, fever, infection, etc. You can also get directions to the nearest hospital or health facility if required.

  • Is a WhatsApp based mother child health telemedicine platform safe and secure?

    Yes, a WhatsApp based mother child health telemedicine platform is safe and secure. All your personal and medical data are encrypted and stored in a secure server. Only authorized doctors and health workers can access your data. Your conversations with the doctor or health worker are also confidential and protected by WhatsApp's end-to-end encryption. You can also delete your messages or data from the platform at any time if you wish.
